About Me

My name is Anthony Kormos, I’m happy to tell you more about myself.

I am a buddening UX Researcher with a background in Business to Business SaaS sales that currently resides in the Washington D.C. Metro area. I just earned my Certificate In UX/UI Design through George Washington/Trilogy’s UX design Bootcamp and am searching for a full-time Junior UX Researcher position.

I received my education from Virginia Commonwealth University’s School of Business with a dual degree in Information Systems and Economics; Cum Laude.

For the past two years, I have been working as a Sales Development Representative within the eCommerce space, cultivating partnerships with household brands like L’Oreal and Puma, as well as small, “mom and pop” sellers alike.

In my current role, I help clients see their business’ potential by crafting narratives about the power of machine learning when unleashed on their historical data.

After spending years learning and talking about technology, I want to refocus my career from selling it to shaping and making technology better for its users and more valuable to the company creating it.

In research, I am energized through truly empathizing with the frustrations of users and it brings me personal satisfaction seeing users feel seen.

Whether in sales or in research, I love speaking with people and understanding what is important to them.

I welcome the next challenge.
